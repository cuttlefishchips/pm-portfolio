The insight
"Users weren't dropping off during onboarding — they were dropping off the moment they realised the app couldn't do the one thing they downloaded it for."

The pain points weren't UX problems. They were feature gap problems. Reframed the entire brief from "improve the flow" to "close the expectation gap."

Pulse · The Chase Figure

It finds you.
You don't find it.

Pulse lives in Telegram. No app to open, no dashboard to check. The conversation interface is the product — so screenshots of the actual chat are the most honest artifact we have.

Pulse replies use teal `--pulse` bubbles — the chase figure accent colour, immediately distinguishable from Telegram's native green.

Insight messages use a darker teal to signal "this is the analysis layer, not just a response."

The phone frame is minimal and dark — draws the eye to the conversation, not the device.
